# 2026-05-20 — Station selection criteria for the curated registry

**What this is:** dated record of how the initial curated registry was chosen; read when
adding stations.

The initial 12-station registry was selected on three criteria: (1) a NOAA CO-OPS harmonic
station within 15 km of a documented tidepooling site, (2) public shore access at the site,
(3) mixed-tide profile with at least monthly sub-0.0 ft daylight lows in summer. Pacific
coverage first (8 stations at launch, 12 by this note); Atlantic pilot pending the
semidiurnal scoring question (see DOMAIN.md East-vs-West note).

Rejected: Gulf-coast stations (diurnal micro-tides — windows exist but the score model's
height factor saturates wrong), Alaska (access), Hawaii (no CO-OPS harmonic coverage at the
candidate sites).
